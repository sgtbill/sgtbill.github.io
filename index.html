Tuesday, 24th July

The bus to Bogotá

With a 22hr journey ahead of me, travelling from the border with Ecuador to Bogotá, I can't decide what I should do about the baby in the row behind me. On the plus side, she's happy at the moment, so isn't crying, however she is kicking my chair a lot, and the parents made a comment when we reclined our seats slightly.

I think I'll just wait it out for a bit, and hopefully she'll fall asleep soon.
